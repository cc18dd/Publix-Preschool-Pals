Sign in to Publix Preschool Pals



January 2010

We've developed an exciting, and free, program for children ages two, three and four and parents alike living in Florida, Georgia, South Carolina, Alabama or Tennessee. It's educational, but it's also entertaining. We call it Publix Preschool Pals.

Here's what you get: information to help your children realize their full potential, access to an information-rich Web site, a quarterly newsletter, prizes, cards on their birthdays, a very cool activity book for two-year-olds, and a one-of-a-kind CD ROM when your children turn three. Please note, the Publix Preschool Pals program is offered in English only.

Why is Publix doing this? Well, each of us at Publix cares about children. We care about their health and happiness. And we care about yours as well. Publix Preschool Pals just seemed like the right thing for us to do.

To sign up for Publix Preschool Pals, all you need to do is complete our registration form. Have fun.
